Best Feature Voting Tools For Product Managers in 2024

By John Doe • May 18, 2025

Feature voting tools help product teams gather feedback directly from users. These tools allow customers to suggest ideas and upvote the ones they want the most.

Why Use Feature Voting Tools?

They help prioritize what to build next based on real user demand, reducing guesswork in product decisions.

Top Feature Voting Tools in 2024

  • Supahub – Clean UI, integrated roadmap, user segmentation
  • Canny – Popular in SaaS, integrated with Intercom
  • Productboard – Best for enterprise teams

Each of these tools has strengths based on your team's size, budget, and feedback process.
