How Parents Can Inspire a Love for Coding

By Olivia Bennett • February 15, 2025

You don’t need to be a tech expert to help your child fall in love with coding. With a little encouragement and curiosity, you can spark a lifelong passion for technology.

Tips for Parents

  • Explore Together: Sit with your child and try simple projects on Scratch.
  • Encourage Creativity: Let them build things they care about—like games or cartoons.
  • Be Supportive: Celebrate effort and experimentation, not just success.

By fostering a tech-positive environment, you're helping build future innovators.
